    Note: kafka-producer and kafka-consumer both use kafka-clients. They are separate plugins for semantic clarity -- a service applies kafka-producer to declare "I produce events" and kafka-consumer to declare "I consume events." This allows future differentiation (e.g., producer-specific config, consumer group management) without breaking the plugin contract.
